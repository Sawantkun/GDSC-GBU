Google Developer Student Clubs

GDSC-GBU serves as a thriving platform that bridges the gap between students and the realm of cutting-edge technology, offering a space to learn, innovate, and collaborate using Google technologies.

ABOUT US

GDSC in Action

GDSC are university based community groups for students interested in Google developer technologies.Students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.It’s aim is to bridge the gap between theory and practical.
